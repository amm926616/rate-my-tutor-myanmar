Welcome to Rate My Tutor - Myanmar!

We believe that students deserve top-quality education from genuine, skilled, and passionate tutors. Too many fake tutors take advantage of students, making it hard to find truly great educators. Our platform helps you discover the real gems in the teaching world – tutors who make a real impact.

With our easy-to-use search system and detailed tutor profiles, you can read reviews, check ratings, and find the perfect tutor for your needs. Together, we can expose fake tutors and celebrate the educators who truly deserve recognition.

  • How does this website work?

Add Passionate Tutors

Come across an amazing tutor who truly loves teaching? You can add their profile to our platform! Just fill in their name, subject, and a brief description of their teaching style. Help undiscovered educators get the recognition they deserve.

Leave Honest Reviews

Help fellow learners by sharing your experiences. Write honest reviews about tutors you've learned from—highlight their strengths and teaching methods so others can make informed choices.

Find the Right Tutor

Looking for a tutor who truly cares about teaching? Explore tutor profiles, read reviews from other students, and find the perfect educator to guide you on your learning journey.

Our Mission

Education should be about passion, not marketing. Too many skilled and dedicated tutors go unnoticed while others profit from flashy promotions. We aim to change that by giving passionate learners a platform to discover and highlight the true educators—the ones who genuinely love to teach. Here, students can add, review, and find tutors based on real experiences, not just advertisements.

Empowering Students

We believe students should have the power to recognize and support tutors who make a real difference. Our platform lets you share and discover top educators.

Giving Tutors Visibility

Passionate tutors often struggle to get noticed. We provide a space where their dedication shines through honest reviews from real learners.

Honest & Transparent Reviews

No paid promotions, no fake ratings—just genuine feedback from students who have learned from these educators.

Helping Learners Find the Best

Whether you're looking for a tutor in a specific subject or someone with a teaching style that suits you, our search and review system helps you find the right fit.
